Faq

1. What do I do when the person paired to me does not pay?

If the person paired to you does not pay after 6 hours of paring, an unpaid button will appear on your dashboard. Click the button to evict the user from the platform then another person will be paired to you.

2. What do i do if i click the unpaid button and end up receiving payment from that person?

This might be possible due to network faliure or delay. Both parties should report the issue to us within 48 hours via our contact page for rectification.

3. What do i do if if i pay but the recipient does not verify my payment?

Leave us a message on our contact page and upload the proof of your payment with the amount, date and time you made payment to the recipient and we will resolve the issue.

4. How do i retrieve my password?

Click on the I forgot my password button on the sign in page and follow the procedure.

5. What do i do if i get evicted wrongfully from this platform?

Although Eviction is permanent, If you have proof that you were wrongfully evicted, leave us a message on our contact page with evidence of your claims, if verified you will be reinstated.

6. Can I alter my profile information?

The only information editable are your bank details, hence it is crucial that you never reveal your password to anyone.
